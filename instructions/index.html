Step 1 - Choose Finger

Choose Index finger or thumb to tap on the buttons. Please stick with your choice throughout the experiment.

Step 2 - Tap on Red Buttons

Tap on the red highlighted button. Once the button is pressed a new button will appear.

Step 3 - Answer Questions

One you have tapped all grids, answer the questions. You can answer a question by clicking on the icons.

Step 4 - Wait for Upload

Your data will be uploaded. Please do not close the app or lock the phone while the data is being uploaded. Done!
